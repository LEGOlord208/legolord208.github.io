termplay

termplay allows you to play videos and images in your terminal!

It can even automatically download YouTube videos for you, and detect playback lag. Why not also make it look better by providing a width/height ratio? Or use the playback format "sixel" in terminals that support them? In other words you can make a really cool looking image still look really cool in your terminal!

But that's not all...

You can pre-process videos for faster viewing. You can customize the frame rate, width, height, and more. Oh, and termplay is completely free and open source, of course.
